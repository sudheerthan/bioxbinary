About BioxBinary

Our Mission

BioxBinary is on a mission to create affordable, intelligent assistive devices that enhance the quality of life for children with disabilities, especially in underserved communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology bridges the gap in care, where every child with special needs has access to tools that promote independence, safety, and well-being.

Our Story

Founded by a team of engineers, healthcare professionals, and parents of children with special needs, BioxBinary was born from firsthand experience with the challenges of medication management and care coordination. We saw a need for smarter solutions that could alleviate the burden on caregivers while empowering children.

How It Works

DoseXTrack makes medication management simple and worry-free in just a few easy steps.

1

Set Up

Load medications into individually sealed compartments and program dosage schedule via the app.

2

Alert

DoseXTrack signals when it's time for medication with customizable sensory-friendly alerts.

3

Dispense

The device automatically dispenses the correct medication in the proper dose.

4

Track

Caregivers receive confirmation and can monitor adherence through the mobile dashboard.

Use Cases

At Home

Helps parents manage complex medication schedules while promoting children's independence and reducing family stress.

At School

Assists school nurses and staff in administering medications accurately to multiple students throughout the day.

In Care Facilities

Supports healthcare providers in maintaining precise medication schedules for children with chronic conditions.

Our Commitment

At BioxBinary, we believe that assistive technology should be accessible to all. We're committed to:

  • Creating affordable solutions that work for families across all income levels
  • Designing with inclusivity in mind to accommodate diverse abilities and needs
