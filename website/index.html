About Us

Our Mission

Our Story

Our Impact

We're addressing critical challenges in healthcare and accessibility.

The Problem We're Solving

Medication errors and non-adherence are leading causes of adverse health outcomes, particularly for individuals with disabilities and older adults. These errors result in approximately 125,000 deaths annually in the US alone and billions in preventable healthcare costs.

For caregivers, managing complex medication regimens creates significant stress and time burden, while for individuals with special needs, the lack of accessible solutions limits independence and dignity.

At bioxbinary, we believe technology should serve everyone, regardless of ability. Our commitment to accessibility and inclusion drives everything we do, from design to implementation.
